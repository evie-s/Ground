Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

What is CBT?

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), according to "Simply Psychology", is a form of talking therapy which can be used to treat people with a wide range of mental health problems Cognitive therapy helps people to develop alternative ways of thinking and behaving which aims to reduce their psychological distress.
